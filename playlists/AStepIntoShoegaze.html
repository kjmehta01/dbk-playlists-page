(A Step Into) Shoegaze

Shoegaze is an elusive term, but it’s a brand of alternative rock that’s heavy on the guitar pedals, as musician Pete Kember wrote for Pitchfork. Fuzzy guitar distortions, ethereal vocals and introspective lyrics melt into dreamy, hypnotic tunes. This playlist pairs ‘90s classics with newer faire coming from the indie rock scene. Best to listen with headphones while staring at the ground.
